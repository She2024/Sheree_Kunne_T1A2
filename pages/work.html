Projects

Current Published work

I have worked for QLD government for over 15 years in a range of roles. I am currently engaged as Content and Communication Officer for QLD Registry of Births, Deaths and Marriages Web Team, while studying Web Development with Coder Academy. I am responsible for the creation, management and continuous improvement of over 200 web assets and PDFs. Our aim is to provide, accessible, correct and easy to understand information about the registries products, services and policies. Some of my recent work includes the addition of, the refund policy to web assets and PDFs, online form email confirmation, and creating accordion content to improve the customer experience.

You can find evidence of work of my work at the Queensland Registry of Births, Deaths and Marriages.

Future release project

The Queensland Registry of Births, Deaths and Marriages, Legislation Review Project is well under way, 24 June 2024 our new legislation will come into effect. I have been working with Web Team to update the registry web assets and PDFs to align with new legislative requirements this includes new form and web asset builds for new services. So watch this space.
